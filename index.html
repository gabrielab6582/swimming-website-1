What is Swimming?

Swimming is the simultaneous movement of the arms and legs to stay above the water

Why is Swimming so Important?

Swimming is a skill that will come useful several time during a person's life.


If it is going to the pool, beach, or any place with water, knowing how to swim will allow to go into the water comfortably and fearlessly. And hey! It might even save your life.
