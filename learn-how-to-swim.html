Learn How to Swim

So Where do You Start

You are probably here because you don't know how to swim. That is perfectly fine. There are a simple few steps that are necessary to take before you start learning to swim. Lets get started!


Step 1 Getting Comfortable in the Water


  • Don't go into deep water right away

  • Let go of any fear of drowning

  • Most importantly never try to learn how to swim without someone who is lifeguard certified


Step 2: Get Used to Floating On Your Back



Holding on the the ledge of the pool and having someone stand next to you, slowly relax back into the water allowing your shoulders and head to recline as if lying down in bed.


When you feel you hips and shoulders to sink, use your hands to lightly scull the water.


Make sure that your next is aligned and parallel with you spine. This is the key to floating.


Step 3: Floating On Your Stomach



When you successfully float on your back without any support,you can move on floating on your stomach.


Stand by the wall with both hands on the ledge of the pool.

Flatten your body so that it is parallel with the water and bottom of the pool.


Make sure your face in looking straight down and your neck is aligned with your spine.



Step 4: Breathing Techniques



The final step before you can start swimming is understanding the breathing technique



Naturally humans breathe in and out through their nose. This is not the case when swimming. Take a breath before entering your head into the water.


When your face is in the water and looking down to the bottom of the pool blow out air through your nose.


This should create a continuous stream of bubbles.


As you rotate your head to the side, without lifting your neck out of the water, take a breath through your mouth and rotate your head back into the water.


Ready to take it to the next level

You are ready to start learning to swim Freestyle! Once you've practiced and feel comfortable with the steps above click the bottom and start the next steps
